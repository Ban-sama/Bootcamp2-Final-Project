About Me

Hello my name is Alex Cruz and this is my story. When I graduated from high school back in 2008 I didn't know what I wanted to do for my future, I had an interest in computers but I was afraid that by pursuing my light interest in computers I would lose interest or come to hate it. It was only after struggling in college for a while that I finally decided to look into learning about computers at ITT Technical Institute. My time at ITT taught me that I enjoyed working with technology and that I would not tire of it like I had orginally feared. While attaining my Associates Degree in "Network Systems Administration" I entered into an internship into the IT divison of the non-profit company "Westcare." I continued to intern at Westcare for 15 months during wich I obtained my Associates Degree, an IC3 certification, and ITT was shut down. After my internship ended I then began looking to enter the workforce and decided to widen my skill set by attending Geekwise's bootcamp courses.
